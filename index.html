Welcome to TeenzUP

Turn your interests into real projects, real experience, and your first income.

TeenzUP connects high school students (16+) with short, paid, interest-based projects ("micro-internships") from modern, socially responsible businesses. Build skills, grow a portfolio, and prove yourself—without needing prior experience.

How TeenzUP Works

1. Create a profile – add skills, interests, and sample work
2. Explore projects – filter by category and what you enjoy
3. Apply in minutes – simple application, clear expectations
4. Complete the project – deliver results, learn on the go
5. Get paid + reviewed – build your reputation for the next opportunity

Why TeenzUP Is Different

Our Unique Advantage: Focus

TeenzUP is not a generic job site. We don't offer "any job." We offer interest-based, short projects that help students earn while doing what they love—building skills that actually matter.

Sustainability

TeenzUP is designed as a social enterprise with a model that doesn't financially burden students. The platform is sustained through:

  • Company service fee (commission): a small fee from businesses for each successfully completed project (fair, results-based).
  • Promoted listings (optional): businesses can pay a modest fee to feature projects and attract more candidates.

Join the Movement

  • "Your talent has value" campaign: real student stories on TikTok & Instagram
  • Online challenges & competitions: logo design, creative video, content ideas—show your talent and get noticed
  • "Invest in the future" for businesses: positioning TeenzUP as a way to support the next generation while solving real tasks
  • First listings free (limited): a low-risk way for companies to try the platform
