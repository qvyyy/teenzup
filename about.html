TeenzUP

About TeenzUP

TeenzUP is an online platform built to empower teenagers and young adults with tools, opportunities, and guidance to succeed in today’s world. We create a bridge between student talent and real business needs—so students can learn by doing and businesses can access fresh ideas.

Our Mission

To help Bulgarian students discover and develop their talents by giving them access to meaningful, interest-based work. We support young people with a safe, structured path toward real experience, confidence, and first earnings.

Our Vision

To become Bulgaria’s leading early-career platform that inspires and supports the next generation of professionals—by turning interests into practical experience and real results.

The Problem We Solve

Many students finish school with strong theory, but:

  • Lack meaningful experience: available jobs are often seasonal, low-skilled, and unrelated to their interests.
  • Face a gap between school and business: students don’t have an easy way to connect with companies that would give them a real chance.

What We Do (Our Solution)

TeenzUP is designed like an ecosystem that supports students in their first professional steps:

  • Interest-based project marketplace: companies post short, paid projects categorized by fields (digital marketing, graphic design, content creation, tech, social causes, and more).
  • Profiles built on skills & talent: students create a profile and portfolio showing what they can do—even without official work experience.
  • Ratings & feedback system: after each project, both sides leave a review to build reputation, accountability, and quality.

Our Values

  • Opportunity with purpose – meaningful work, not “just a job”
  • Growth & creativity – skill-building through real challenges
  • Inclusion & community – a space where every teen can thrive
  • Trust & responsibility – transparency through feedback and clear expectations
